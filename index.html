A Bit About Myself

Hi! I'm Rijan Buddhacharya, a passionate developer and BIM (Bachelor of Information Management) student currently in my 7th semester. I hail from the historically rich city of Bhaktapur, Nepal — a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its ancient art and architecture.
